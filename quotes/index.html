Shrek Quotes

"Ogres are like onions." - Shrek

I obviously have to start with probably the most quoted Shrek quote of all time, "Ogres are like onions." This was said by Shrek to Donkey when they were walking through the corn field. Shrek was trying to explain how ogres are more than just they appear, they are complex creatures with LAYERS!

"What are you doing in my SWAMP?" - Shrek

This is another very classic Shrek quote. Shrek yells this at all of the fairy tale creatures who have been dumped in HIS SWAMP by Lord Farquaad. I think this is just a fun line to say if you try to imitate Shrek.

"Do you know the muffin man?" - Gingy

I know I already covered this in Gingy sections of the character page, but I feel that this is just such a great scene it deserves to be mentioned again. This is an incredibly funny back and forth between Gingy and Lord Farquaad where they are talking about knowing the Muffin Man.

"Better out than in, I always say!" - Shrek

This is just another classic and funny quote. But throughout the movie, it plays a much bigger role than just being funny. It shows that Fiona and Shrek are actually more alike than they think. This is used as a great way to progress their relationship. Not only does this play a role in Shrek and Fiona's relationship, it's also a part of the second movie. When Fiona and Shrek are having dinner with Harold and Lillian, Shrek says this line when Fiona burps. This really shows how different Shrek and Fiona are from Fiona's parents, and it reinforces Harold's initial beliefs and disliking of Shrek.

"Well that's not very nice. It's just a donkey." - Shrek

This is from when Farquaad says "What's that? It's hideous!" This was an attack at Shrek, but by saying this Shrek made it about Donkey.

"And in the morning, I'm making waffles!" - Donkey

This is probably my favorite Donkey quotes. Donkey says this to Shrek when they first get to Shrek's swamp. This line is just so fun and I think it shows how fun Donkey is. This line is also refrenced in the forth movie when Donkey goes to try and eat some waffles that are actually a trap set by the rogue ogres.

"If that was me, you'd be dead." - Shrek

This is the scene where Donkey thinks Shrek made a stinky fart. But in reality, it was the sulfer from the volcano. Shrek says this because if it was him making the smell, Donkey would pass out because of how bad the stench is.

"I'm an ogre, ROAR!" - Donkey

This is when Shrek and Fiona are having a fight about weather to stay in Far Far Away. Shrek says "I'm an ogre" and then roars at a little dog. Donkey immitates Shrek for this because Shrek was kidna being a jerk by doing that and he deserved to be made of.

"Cute, button nose? Thick, wavy locks? Taut, round buttocks?" - Shrek

I think this is a great quote because it shows how attractive Shrek is in human form. This is when Shrek wakes up to a bunch of girls all around him after he fell asleep from drinking that potion. When he woke up he realized that he was turned into a human and he thought that would make Fiona really like him and have a happily ever after. Donkey was turned into a noble steed and it's believed that Dragon was turned into a pegasus.

"Donkey, you have the right to remain silent... what you lack is the capacity." - Shrek

This is from when Shrek and Donkey were getting arrested because they were accused for stealing by Fairy Godmother when they overheard that Prince Charming is her son. Donkey kept saying that no one told him he as the right to remain silent, so Shrek made this joke.
