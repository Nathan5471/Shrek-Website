Shrek Characters

Welcome to the Shrek Characters page where you can learn more about all of your favorite characters from the Shrek and Puss in Boots movies.

Shrek

Shrek is the main character of the Shrek movies, oviously. He is a big, green, scary (not scary to me!) ogre who live in a SWAMP! Shrek shows a lot of character progression throughout the films, because at the start he was not willing to make friends, but in the end he became best friends with Donkey and Puss in Boots, and married Fiona.

Donkey

Donkey is Shrek's bestest friend ever! He started out as an annoying talking donkey (again, not annoying to me!) who really annoyed Shrek, but throughout the first move he became Shrek's friend. Later he also becomes best friends with Puss in Boots. Dokney even married the dragon from the first film and had 4 donkey-dragon hybrid babies with her.

Fiona

Fiona is Shrek's wife and a princess from the kingdom of Far Far Away. She started out the first film as a human princess locked away in a castle guarded by a terrible fire-breathing dragon, that had an enchantment upon her of a fearful sort which could only be broken by true love's first kiss. She was rescued by Shrek and Donkey, but she thought Shrek was an ugly ogre (I think he's hot!) and thought that Farquaad was her true love. After spending a bunch of time with Shrek, she realized how hot, amazing, and funny Shrek is. Later she kissed Shrek and she was turned into an ogre permanently and then she got married to Shrek!

Puss in Boots (aka Pickles)

Puss in Boots is our favorite fearless hero from the Shrek movies. He is even featured in his own spin-off movies! Puss in Boots was hired to kill Shrek by Fiona's father, King Harold, but he ended up become Shrek and Donkey's best friend because Shrek spared his life. He also has a really cool catchphrase: "Fear me, if you dare," where he also cuts his signature mark. In Puss in Boots: The Last Wish, he became friends with Perrito, and he picked up the nickname "Pickles." Puss is now down to only 1 of his 9 lives, so he has to live this one to the fullest.
