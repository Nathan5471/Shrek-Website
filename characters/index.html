Welcome to the Shrek Characters page where you can learn more about all of your favorite characters from the Shrek and Puss in Boots movies.

Shrek

Shrek is the main character of the Shrek movies, oviously. He is a big, green, scary (not scary to me!) ogre who live in a SWAMP! Shrek shows a lot of character progression throughout the films, because at the start he was not willing to make friends, but in the end he became best friends with Donkey and Puss in Boots, and married Fiona.
